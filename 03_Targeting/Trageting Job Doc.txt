## Expanding Search: Music Production / Audio Engineering (Austin, TX & Atlanta, GA)
I’ve started exploring roles in the music and audio industry since you're open to lower-paying gigs or internships to get your foot in the door in Austin or Atlanta.

**Atlanta, GA Opportunities**
1. **Patchwerk Recording Studios (Atlanta, GA)** - Internship Program
   - *Overview*: Patchwerk is a legendary hip-hop and R&B studio that offers formal, highly competitive internship programs for aspiring Audio Engineers.
   - *Requirements*: Often requires a background/degree in recording arts or active enrollment, plus local residency and a car.
2. **Bravo Ocean Studios (Atlanta, GA)** - Studio Intern / Assistant Engineer
   - *Overview*: They periodically hire interns/assistants to shadow sessions (music, podcast, film audio). Keep an eye out for openings.
3. *Broader Atlanta Scope*: Major labels, production houses, and live venues consistently hire "A/V Integration Technicians" and "Audio Visual Assistants" as entry points into live sound.

**Austin, TX Opportunities**
1. **Live Sound / Venue Production Assist**
   - *Overview*: Places like Zach Theatre, Zilker Hillside Theatre, Stubb's, and other major live music venues hire audio engineering assistants or live-sound interns, especially leading into festival season.
2. **Local Studio Outreach**
   - *Studios*: The Bubble, Arlyn Studios, Pony Sound.
   - *Strategy*: These studios rarely post on job boards like Indeed. The standard approach is to email the Studio Manager directly with your portfolio and resume, expressing interest in assisting/interning.
3. **University of Texas (UT) Network**
   - *Overview*: UT's Moody College has deep ties to the industry. Studios nearby often hire assistants.

**Next Steps & Recommendations for Audio Roles:**
- **Portfolio:** Ensure you have a solid portfolio/reel showcasing your DAW (Ableton, Pro Tools, Logic) experience, beats, or mixed tracks.
- **Cold Emailing:** For both Austin and Atlanta recording studios, the best way to land a job is by compiling a list of 10-15 local studios and direct-emailing their Studio Managers with an offer to assist, intern, or shadow.
- **Job Boards:** Keep "Audio Visual Integration" and "AV Technician" searches active, as those IT-adjacent roles often overlap with your current support engineering skills and can serve as a stepping stone into dedicated studio live sound.
