# Nexus WiFi — Gap Prompts

Updated: 2026-03-11 (Phase 2.1)

Many gaps from the original 30 questions have been resolved by user input. The remaining questions below are optional — they would further strengthen interview stories but are NOT required for resume generation.

---

## RESOLVED (do not re-ask)

- Common issue categories: downed devices/infrastructure, internet outages, device connection issues, general support
- Diagnostic flow: clarify → scope → check downed devices → troubleshoot → reference tickets → escalate
- Ticket system: in-house internal platform via company website
- Volume: ~2-5/night, ~5-10/morning plus outbound calling (rough estimates)
- Technical exposure: camera setup, equipment troubleshooting, hardware replacement coordination, basic DHCP verification
- Role framing: foundational-to-intermediate support, not network engineering
- Protocol-level stories: not enough specific recall — do not invent

## REMAINING — Optional for Interview Prep

These are only needed if you want stronger STAR stories. Short 1-2 sentence answers are fine.

### Equipment / Triage

1. Can you recall a specific time you identified a failed piece of equipment (AP, switch, gateway, modem) and what happened next?

2. What does the hardware replacement coordination process look like? Do you submit a request, call someone, update a ticket?

### Customer Interaction

3. Can you think of a time a customer was particularly frustrated? What did you do?

4. Do you mostly interact with property managers, individual tenants, or both?

5. Have you ever onboarded a new property? What did that involve?

### Operations

6. How do you prioritize between new incoming tickets and ongoing ones during a shift?

7. Do you do anything to track or manage your own workload beyond the ticketing system?

### Impact / Recognition

8. Has a manager or customer ever recognized you for something specific? What was it?

9. Is there a moment at Nexus WiFi you're proud of, even if it seems small?

### Team

10. Have you trained or helped onboard any newer team members?
