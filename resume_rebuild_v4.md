  2. "Triage connectivity outages and conduct fault isolation across access points, switches..." (Integrates specific hardware directly into the context of the work)
  3. "Maintain outbound communication with non-technical users..." (Demonstrates customer communication skills)
* **3 Weakest Remaining Gaps:**
  1. No specific enterprise infrastructure networking tools listed (e.g., Zabbix, Azure Active Directory).
  2. Brief tenure in the insurance role.
  3. Records "coursework" rather than a completed degree.
* **3 Likely Recruiter Objections:**
  1. "Does he have sufficient core networking knowledge for an escalation role?"
  2. "Will he need relocation support to move to Austin?"
